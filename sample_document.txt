Company Leave Policy - 2024
===========================

1. Annual Leave
   All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
   Leave must be applied at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal.
   Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days to the next year.

2. Sick Leave
   Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
   A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
   Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

3. Maternity & Paternity Leave
   Maternity leave: 26 weeks of paid leave for the primary caregiver.
   Paternity leave: 2 weeks of paid leave for the secondary caregiver.
   Must be applied 4 weeks before the expected date.

4. Public Holidays
   Employees are entitled to all government-declared public holidays.
   If required to work on a public holiday, employees receive double pay.

5. Work From Home Policy
   Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
   Core working hours are 10 AM to 4 PM regardless of location.

6. Leave Without Pay
   Employees may apply for leave without pay for personal reasons.
   Maximum leave without pay allowed is 30 days per year.

For any queries contact hr@company.com
